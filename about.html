About Me

My name is Kyle Noble. I am from Portland, Oregon, and I graduated from Oregon State University in June 2021 with a Bachelor's Degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a minor in Computer Science. The courses that I enjoyed the most generally involve computer microarchitecture, operating systems, VLSI, embedded systems, multimedia, and computer networking. I am currently pursuing career opportunities in software engineering, embedded programming, and computer engineering. I am passionate about learning and challenging myself, so I try to undertake new problems and expand the breadth and depth of my knowledge.
